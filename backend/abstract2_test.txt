Advances in deep learning have transformed object detection, yet challenges remain in low‑light and occluded environments. We present a novel convolutional–transformer hybrid model that leverages spatial attention to enhance detection accuracy under adverse conditions. Evaluated on benchmark datasets, our approach achieves superior performance compared to state‑of‑the‑art baselines, particularly in scenarios with high visual noise. These findings highlight the potential of attention‑driven architectures for robust computer vision applications.
